# HeartRateGraph Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

## Summary

HeartRateGraph is an app for monitoring heart rate during exercise. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

## Data the app uses

HeartRateGraph processes the following data:

- **Heart rate (BPM):** received in real time from a Bluetooth heart rate sensor/strap compatible with the Bluetooth Heart Rate Service standard.
- **Bluetooth device identifier:** the address of the paired sensor, used only to automatically reconnect to your favorite sensor.
- **Age and resting heart rate:** values you enter manually to calculate your training zones.
- **Session chart snapshots:** if you choose to save them, they are stored as images in your local gallery/storage.

## How this data is stored

**All the data described above is processed and stored exclusively on your device.** HeartRateGraph has no servers of its own, does not send any data over the internet, does not use analytics or remote crash-reporting services, and does not share information with third parties.

Data is stored locally through the app's preferences (SharedPreferences) and, for chart snapshots, through your Android device's standard image storage.

## Permissions requested by the app

- **Bluetooth (scanning and connection):** required to find and connect to your heart rate strap.
- **Location (only on older Android versions, 11 or below):** Android requires this permission as a technical requirement to scan for Bluetooth devices on those system versions. HeartRateGraph does not use your geographic location in any way, nor does it store or transmit it.

## Health data

The heart rate data shown by the app is an approximation based on standard training-zone formulas (Tanaka, Karvonen) and the information from your Bluetooth sensor. **This app is not a medical device**, does not diagnose or treat any medical condition, and should not be used as a substitute for advice from a healthcare professional.

## Children

HeartRateGraph is not specifically directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the app adds new features. Any change will be reflected on this same page, updating the date shown above.
